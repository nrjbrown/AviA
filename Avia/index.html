AviA - AviAtion on the Blockchain

AviA is a decentralized application (Dapp) for the aviation industry. This Dapp seamlessly integrates manufactures, airlines, regulatory bodies and the aviation resale market in one instance. In effect creating a decentralized, globally accessible, source of an aeroplane's records and it's components for transparency, immutability, audibility and trust.

AviAtion Dapp

Manufacturers

Airplane parts and components are registered on the Ethereum Blockchain after they are manufactured together with all relevant data (E.g.serial codes)

Airlines

When airline aquirer these components and with the help of IoTs we are able to track and log them including what has been done to these components. (E.g.Maintainence, Repairs and Overhauls.) Which can also help determine resale values

Auditor

Regulatory bodies such as the FAA, TC, ICAO now have a single global source to audit airworthiness aspects such as regular inspections and maintenence set out by the manufacture (eg. engine hours)
